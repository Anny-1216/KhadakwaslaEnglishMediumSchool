About Us

Our Legacy

Established with a vision to provide quality education, Khadakwasla English Medium School (KEMS) has been serving the community in Khadakwadi with excellence in education. Our school focuses on holistic development of students through a perfect blend of academics and extra-curricular activities.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that enable them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in education that nurtures talent, fosters creativity, and builds character.
